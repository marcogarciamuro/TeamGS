What is TeamGS?

Team GS, short for Team Gumshoe, is a website that provides a central space where you can:

  • Follow the teams that you support
  • View a team's scheduled/previous games
  • Check a team's rank in their respective league or conference
  • Read what the world of sports is saying about a team in news articles
  • Talk to fellow fans about a team

The data and information that this website displays was retreived using the APIs: API-Football, API-NBA, and NewsAPI.

The drive for creating this website was my strong passion for sports. I am a huge fan of soccer and basketball and I wanted to create a service that would inform similar fans about these sports and the teams they like. More specifcally, the creation of this site came about because of previous inconveniences and annoyances. I was constantly Googling when a certain team played, and when this other certain team played, or if there were any live games I could watch at that current time. This sparked an idea to create a space where people could see easily track and view information about the teams/sports they regularly watch or support.
